Druid 0.9.1.1 release

by Druid PMC · June 28, 2016

We're excited to announce the general availability of our latest release, Druid 0.9.1.1!

Druid 0.9.1.1 contains hundreds of performance improvements, stability improvements, and bug fixes over the previous 0.9.0 release, from over 30 contributors. Major new features include an experimental Kafka indexing service to support exactly-once consumption from Apache Kafka, support for cluster-wide query-time lookups (QTL), and an improved segment balancing algorithm.

You can download the release here: /downloads.html

The full release notes are here: https://github.com/apache/incubator-druid/releases/druid-0.9.1.1
